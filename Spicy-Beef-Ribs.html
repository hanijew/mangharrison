About This Dish

Mang Harrison's Spicy Beef Ribs are a mouthwatering tribute to bold Filipino flavors. The ribs are marinated in a zesty blend of chili, garlic, soy, and secret seasonings, then slow-braised until tender. The result is a rich, fiery dish that's balanced with hints of sweetness and depth — a must-try for spice lovers.
